Why does our mission affect you

A bit about who we are. MarkMe was setup up by two friends after our A2 exams 2018. We both found the same problem coming into our exams.

    "Our teachers do alot for us, but on a personal level, I need something more"

We both tried other websites to see if there was extre tutoring or something similar to help boost our preporations for the exams. But, either they were extremely expensive for not a wide range of support or the help was almost useless. There was always the option for private tutoring, however this wasn't an option for either of us.


My exam period was hard, really hard. I had 2 essay subjects and a coursework dominant subject. The problem was that I needed more support with past papers, I needed them to be marked more often before I sat the real thing. My teacher however had 20 other students in my class alone to help. Thats where the idea for MarkMe started with a friend who had similar issues

Cameron Cook Co-Founder MarkMe

I have always struggled with my exams, and I couldn't find the support that I needed all the time. We strive to create a support base to help all students achieve their goals.
